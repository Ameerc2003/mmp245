Episodes

season 1

1. Pilot

The Yu-Gi-Oh! pilot episode introduces Yugi Mutou, a high school student who solves the Millennium Puzzle, unleashing the spirit of an ancient Pharaoh.

2. The Gauntlet Is Thrown

Yugi's alter ego, Yami Yugi, continues to face off against Seto Kaiba, a ruthless duelist who seeks to claim Yugi's powerful Blue-Eyes White Dragon card.

3.Journey To The Duelist Pilot

Yugi faces Weevil Underwood in a duel where Weevil throws Yugi's Exodia cards into the ocean. Yugi must find a way to win without them.

4. Into The Hornet's Nest

Yugi duels Rex Raptor, who uses powerful dinosaur-themed cards. Yugi must adapt his strategy to overcome Rex's fierce creatures.

5. The Ultimate Great Moth

Yugi faces the illusionist, "The Mystical Elf," in a magical duel. Yugi must outwit his opponent's tricks to emerge victorious.

6. First Duel

Yugi's grandfather, Solomon, faces the villainous Pegasus in a Shadow Game. Yugi and his friends must find a way to save Solomon's soul.

7. Attack from the Deep

Yugi duels against Seto Kaiba's brother, Mokuba, who seeks revenge for his brother's defeat. Yugi must overcome Mokuba's determination to win.

8. Everything's Relative

Yugi faces off against Seto Kaiba once again, this time in a rematch with higher stakes. Yugi must use all his skill and courage to defeat Kaiba and protect his friends.
